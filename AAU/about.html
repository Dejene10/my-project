ABOUT

      Addis Ababa University ( AAU) ( Amharic: አዲስ አበባ ዩኒቨርሲቲ) is a large, 
        highly residential national university in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. 
        The university is the oldest school of higher education in Ethiopia. AAU has thirteen campuses. 
        Twelve of these are situated in Addis Ababa, and one is located in Bishoftu, about 45 kilometres (28 mi) away.
        Campus: Addis Ababa (14 campus, including main)
        Location: Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.
        President: Professor Tassew Woldehanna
        Students: 48,673 (2013/14)
